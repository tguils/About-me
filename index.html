About me

Hi! My name is Tess and I'm studying to be a Web Developer. I have spent my professional life so far as a Social Worker, helping families and individuals access resources and support in challenging times. Helping people has always been a passion of mine, and so has solving problems. I have always enjoyed working on formulas and making a final product through tinkering. This lead me to start coding and trying to create my own websites. The learning process has been extremely fun for me.
